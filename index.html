About Us

Africa’s premier online stack institute

Ustacky is an edtech platform that teaches industry-relevant programming skills.

Based in Lagos - Nigeria, it is the first of its kind on the African continent. We leverage cutting-edge technological solutions to deliver the finest software development courses.

We stand out with an expert-led custom syllabus that makes coding as easy as pie. Watch out for curated contents conceived and delivered by African instructors for a primarily African audience.

We have a gender diversity policy. By providing incentives that encourage women to take up and advance careers in software development, we bridge the male-female tech workforce disparity in Africa and the world at large.

Africa’s dream as the future tech talent hub inches closer towards reality with Ustacky.

Why Ustacky?

Learning is an essential part of professional development. And it’s even more imperative in STEM.

We have created a platform that leverages internet technology to get you started and/or further a career in software development and related fields.

The Ustacky team brings a cumulative 20-year software development experience to the fore with careers spanning multiple industries and countries. Our award-winning team boasts premium certifications confirming their pedagogical prowess in teaching software development courses.

Being the pioneer online stack learning platform in Africa only confers more responsibility on us.
